Genome Browser For E. coli BW25113 x Phage
DubSeq Data

This genome browser shows the effects of overexpressed genome fragments on phage susceptibility in E. coli strain BW25113, using data from experiments in which a "DubSeq" overexpression library was challenged with various phage. This log-fold-change data is quite noisy, likely because of secondary phage resistance mutations associated with cells containing particular fragments, but the top hits are often supported by many fragments, suggesting that the gene in question provides an advantage when overexpressed. To see the top and bottom hits for each phage, click on its name on the left. To change the set of phage displayed, or show the underlying fragment count and fitness data, click the buttons above the heatmap track. Hover over any gene or fitness measurement for more information, and click to see more information about that gene.
